What We Offer

Industrial-Grade Monitoring Services

ARM & PLC-based IoT solutions tailored for your specific needs — from single installations to full site automation.

💧

Water Tank Monitoring

Real-time ultrasonic level sensing for overhead/underground tanks. Auto motor control with overflow protection and SMS/app alerts.

  • ARM-based controller with ultrasonic level sensing
  • Auto pump ON/OFF at set thresholds
  • Overflow & dry-run protection
  • Mobile app live dashboard
⚙️

Motor Health Monitoring

Continuous health tracking of pumps and motors. Detect temperature spikes, overcurrent, and vibration anomalies before they cause downtime.

  • Temperature & current sensing
  • Run-time hour logging
  • Fault detection with auto-shutdown
  • Predictive maintenance alerts
⚡

Energy Usage Tracking

Monitor your electricity consumption in real-time. Identify wastage, track peak hours, and generate monthly usage reports automatically.

  • Smart energy meter with ARM-based data acquisition
  • kWh, voltage & current logging
  • Daily/monthly reports on WhatsApp
  • High consumption alerts
